Killer Scents

Graphic Design project based on a company I created

I created the logo and name for a candle company. The idea behind this was to create a campaign based on the made-up company and make social media posts and merch that you would create for a client.

  • Assets

  • Candle Mockup - Label created in Illustrator, brought into Photoshop to create the candle.
  • Shirt Mockup - Logos Designed in Illustrator, brought into photoshop to look screen printed.

  • Magazine Mockup

I brought a multitde of assets together to create this magazine mockup. I first took the candle mockup I had previously made, and added it to a table from unsplash. I then brought in my own image and placed it above the candle. From there, I used photoshop brushes of smoke and created a smoke effect from the candle, and added mask layers to create the effect of a smokey image appearing from the candle. My original photo was blue, so I changed the colouring to give it an orange appearance to fit the colour scheme. I also painted in a shadow from the candle onto the table to make it look a little more realistic. I then added the text and exported that magazine cover as its own png.
Once the cover was created, I brought it into photoshop once again in order to add it to the magagine. I warped the png to the shape of the page, covering the original image in order to give it a realistic curve. Since there was a shadow on the magazine itself, I edited the lighting of the cover and then painted on another shadow to match the original image. Finally, I touched up some of the edges in the middle.
