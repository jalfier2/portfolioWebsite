Altheda Resort

A resort I created for my web development class..

I started with an idea to make a fully-functioning hotel website, then it turned to a resort with a gift shop. It is fully functioning, you can book a room, order items from the gift shop, book a spa or other anemities, and more. I spent a very long time working on the site and was very proud of the overall result.
